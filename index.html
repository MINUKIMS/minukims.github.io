Welcome to my page!
I am a Master's student in the SSSC Lab at KAIST (Statistical Speech and Sound Computing Laboratory), advised by Prof. Hoirin Kim.

My academic interests lie at the intersection of linguistics and artificial intelligence. In particular, I enjoy exploring phonetics, phonology, and language typology, and I am passionate about connecting linguistic theories with computational approaches.

My research focuses on applying this interdisciplinary perspective to low-resource language processing and multilingual automatic speech recognition (ASR).
